# Features Guide 🎮

Complete documentation of MindSpark Quest features and how to use them.

---

## 1. Welcome & Onboarding 👋

### Purpose
Get to know the child and personalize their experience.

### Features
- **Name Entry**: Child enters their adventurer name
- **Age Group Selection**: Choose between "Ages 5-7" or "Ages 8-10"
  - *Ages 5-7*: Visual emphasis, audio cues, simpler reflection prompts
  - *Ages 8-10*: Strategic text reflection, deeper habit exploration
- **Parent Access**: Quick button to switch to Parent Dashboard

### User Experience
- Large, friendly typography (dyslexia-friendly fonts)
- Warm, welcoming color scheme
- Simple, uncluttered interface

---

## 2. Interactive Island Map 🗺️

### Purpose
Central hub showing all 16 Habits of Mind organized by school values.

### How It Works

#### Island Organization
The island is divided into **5 biomes**, each representing a school value:

| Biome | Value | Characters | Colors |
|-------|-------|-----------|--------|
| 🔍 **Whispering Woods** | Curiosity | Sprout, Atlas, Mirror, Scout, Nova, Spark | Greens & Blues |
| 🤝 **Harmony Hive** | Collaboration | Harmony, Luna, Crystal | Warm Golds |
| 🎨 **Dreamers Peak** | Creativity | Prism, Flexi, Giggles | Vibrant Purples |
| 💚 **Gentle Valley** | Compassion | Sage, Luna, Flexi, Mirror, Scout | Soft Pinks & Peaches |
| 🦁 **Brave Cliffs** | Courage | Rocky, Brave, Archer, Sage, Sprout | Bold Reds & Oranges |

#### Daily Quest Highlight
- **Today's Featured Habit**: One character is specially highlighted each day
- **Rotation Cycle**: Changes daily in a 16-day cycle
- **Visual Indication**: Golden glow, animated border, or special marker
- **Consistency Mechanic**: Encourages daily habit exploration

#### Interactive Elements
- **Click to Select**: Tap any character to view their habit and mini-quest
- **Visual Feedback**: Hover states show exploration options
- **Progress Indicator**: Completed quests show different visual style
- **Navigation**: Easy return to island from any quest screen

## 3. Deep Story Quests 📖

### Currently Featured Stories

#### 🐐 Rocky the Goat - Perseverance
**Habit**: Persisting through challenges

**Quest Structure**:
1. **Introduction**: Rocky faces a mountain climbing challenge
2. **Narrative Choices**: 
   - Choice A: "Take the easy path around"
   - Choice B: "Climb the mountain carefully"
   - Choice C: "Ask Rocky's friend for help"
3. **Consequences**: Each choice leads to different story outcomes
4. **Reflection**: "When do YOU stick with something hard?"
5. **Breathing Mechanic**: Guided breathing exercise between scenes

**Rewards**:
- 🎫 Gold Sticker for completion
- 💎 25 MindSpark Gems
- 🌱 Growth Garden plant

---

#### 🦉 Sage the Owl - Asking Questions & Curiosity
**Habit**: Asking effective questions to understand

**Quest Structure**:
1. **Introduction**: Sage discovers a mysterious forest puzzle
2. **Question Prompts**: Interactive scenarios requiring questions
3. **Narrative Choices**: Select which questions to ask
4. **Story Development**: Questions reveal puzzle pieces
5. **Reflection**: "What questions help you understand better?"

**Rewards**:
- 🎫 Silver Sticker for completion
- 💎 25 MindSpark Gems
- 🌱 Growth Garden plant

---

#### 🐘 Luna the Elephant - Teamwork & Empathy
**Habit**: Working together with others

**Quest Structure**:
1. **Introduction**: Luna needs to organize a forest gathering
2. **Collaboration Scenarios**: Make choices that require teamwork
3. **Empathy Moments**: Consider others' feelings and perspectives
4. **Team Building**: See how cooperation solves problems
5. **Reflection**: "How do you work best with friends?"

**Rewards**:
- 🎫 Copper Sticker for completion
- 💎 25 MindSpark Gems
- 🌱 Growth Garden plant

---

### Standard Quest Flow

```
Story Setup
    ↓
Character Narrative + SVG Art
    ↓
Choice Presentation (2-3 options)
    ↓
Child Makes Selection
    ↓
Story Progression / Consequence
    ↓
Breathing Exercise (optional)
    ↓
Reflection Prompt
    ↓
Reward Screen
```

## 4. Growth Garden 🌱

### Purpose
Visual representation of habit development and consistency.

### Mechanics

#### Plant Growth
- **One Plant per Completed Quest**: Each habit completion grows one plant
- **Visual Progression**: 3-stage plant lifecycle (seedling → sprout → bloom)
- **Persistent Growth**: Garden grows throughout the year
- **Seasonal Themes**: Visual changes based on season

#### Garden Navigation
- **View Full Garden**: Access from island map or parent dashboard
- **Individual Plant Profiles**: Click to see quest history for that habit
- **Share Screenshot**: (Optional) Take garden snapshots to show progress

#### Motivational Aspects
- **Visual Progress**: See growth accumulate over time
- **Sense of Accomplishment**: Beautiful garden visually celebrates success
- **Encouragement to Explore**: Empty garden spots inspire trying new quests

## 5. Sticker Collection 🎫

### Purpose
Achievement tracking and rewarding consistent engagement.

### Collection System

#### Sticker Types
- **Gold Stickers**: First quest completion of each habit
- **Silver Stickers**: Completion with excellent choices
- **Copper Stickers**: Standard completion
- **Gem Stones**: Special achievements (rare)

#### Sticker Display
- **Collection Gallery**: View all earned stickers
- **Sticker Info**: Tap to see quest date and choices made
- **Share & Print**: Print sticker collection to display at home

#### Achievement Unlocks
- **5 Stickers**: "Curious Adventurer" badge
- **10 Stickers**: "Habit Explorer" badge
- **All 16 Stickers**: "Wisdom Seeker" badge

---

## 6. MindSpark Gems 💎

### Purpose
Accumulated points reflecting total engagement and consistency.

### Gem Mechanics

#### Earning Gems
- **Base Reward**: 25 gems per completed quest
- **Bonus for Choices**: +5 gems for thoughtful reflection
- **Streak Bonus**: +10 gems for completing quests on consecutive days
- **First Quest**: 50 gems as welcome bonus

#### Using Gems (Future Feature)
- Unlock special character outfits
- Customize garden themes
- Access bonus mini-games

#### Gem Counter
- **Always Visible**: Top of screen shows current gem count
- **Animation**: Gems increase with rewards
- **Milestone Celebrations**: Special animation at 50, 100, 250 gems

## 7. Daily Rotation System 📅

### Purpose
Encourage daily engagement and systematic exploration of all 16 habits.

### How It Works

#### Rotation Cycle
```
Day 1: Rocky (Perseverance)
Day 2: Sage (Questioning)
Day 3: Luna (Teamwork)
Day 4: Harmony (Communication)
Day 5: Crystal (Clarity)
... (continues through all 16)
Day 16: Spark (Wonder)
Then repeats...
```

#### Daily Quest Highlight
- **Every 24 Hours**: Featured quest changes automatically
- **Visual Marker**: Golden glow on today's character
- **Quick Access**: "Today's Quest" button on island
- **Consistency Reward**: Come back daily for streak bonuses

#### First Visit Check
- System automatically detects first visit to app
- Welcome with onboarding
- Subsequent visits show progress

## 8. Reflection Prompts 💭

### Purpose
Encourage children to think about how habits apply to their own lives.

### Reflection Types

#### Post-Quest Reflections
After completing a story, children see:
- **Open-ended question**: "How is this habit like something in your life?"
- **Multiple perspectives**: 3 different reflection angles
- **Optional voice recording**: (For ages 8-10) Record thoughts
- **Journal entry**: (Optional) Parent can write notes

#### Parent-Guided Reflections
In Parent Dashboard:
- "Conversation Starters" for each habit
- Suggested questions to ask your child
- Activity ideas for deeper exploration

## 10. Breathing & Mindfulness 🧘

### Purpose
Teach children calm regulation and present-moment awareness.

### Features

#### Guided Breathing Exercise
- **Visual Animation**: Expanding/contracting circle guides breath
- **Audio Option**: Soft chime guides breath timing
- **Duration**: 1-2 minute exercise
- **Accessibility**: Works without sound

#### When It Appears
- Optional between story scenes
- Suggested after emotional/intense moments
- Quick access button on any screen
- Customizable frequency

#### Breathing Patterns
- **Calm Breathing**: 4-4-4 pattern (inhale-hold-exhale)
- **Energizing Breath**: 4-2-6 pattern
- **Focusing Breath**: Box breathing (4-4-4-4)

## 12. Data Persistence 💾

### What Gets Saved
- Child profile (name, age, join date)
- Completed quests and dates
- Choices made in each story
- Sticker collection
- Gem count
- Garden plant growth
- Reflection responses

### How It Works
- **Automatic Saving**: After each action
- **Local Storage**: Browser's built-in storage
- **No Internet Needed**: Works completely offline
- **Persistent Across Sessions**: Data survives browser close/reopen

### Data Privacy
- ✅ No data sent to servers
- ✅ All data stored on device only
- ✅ Can be manually exported
- ✅ Can be manually deleted
