# Getting Started 🚀

Quick start guide for playing MindSpark Quest.

---

## Installation

### Great News! 🎉
**No installation required!** MindSpark Quest is a web app that runs directly in your browser.

### System Requirements
- ✅ Any modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- ✅ No internet connection needed (works offline)
- ✅ Any device with a browser (laptop, tablet, smartphone)
- ✅ No special plugins or extensions required

---

## How to Play

### Option 1: Direct File Opening (Easiest)

1. **Navigate to the folder** where you saved MindSpark Quest
2. **Find `index.html`** file
3. **Double-click** it
4. Your browser will open and the app will load automatically

### Option 2: Right-Click Open

1. **Right-click** on `index.html`
2. Select **"Open with"** → Choose your preferred browser
3. Press **Enter**

### Option 3: Browser Menu

1. **Open your web browser**
2. Press **Ctrl+O** (Windows) or **Cmd+O** (Mac)
3. **Navigate to and select** `index.html`
4. Click **Open**

## First Time Setup

### Step 1: Welcome Screen
When you open the app, you'll see a friendly welcome screen.

**What to do:**
- Enter your adventurer name in the text box
- Select your age group (5-7 or 8-10)
- Click **"Enter the Island"**

### Step 2: Explore the Island
You'll see a beautiful map with 16 animal characters organized by color:
- 🔍 **Green zone**: Curiosity habits
- 🤝 **Gold zone**: Collaboration habits
- 🎨 **Purple zone**: Creativity habits
- 💚 **Pink zone**: Compassion habits
- 🦁 **Red zone**: Courage habits

### Step 3: Complete Your First Quest
- **Click on any character** to start their story quest
- **Or look for today's highlighted quest** (golden glow)
- Follow the story and make your choices
- Complete the reflection prompt
- Earn your first sticker and gems!

## Understanding Your First Quest

### Story Screens
Each quest shows:
- 📖 **Character & Story**: The adventure narrative
- 🎨 **Character Art**: Beautiful SVG illustration
- 💭 **Your Choices**: 2-3 options to choose from

### Making Choices
1. **Read the story carefully**
2. **Think about what the character might do**
3. **Click your choice**
4. Watch the story unfold based on your decision

### Reflection
After the story ends:
1. You'll see a **reflection prompt**
2. Think about how this habit applies to YOUR life
3. You can **type notes** or just think about it
4. Click **Continue** to see your rewards

## Data & Privacy

### What Gets Saved?
✅ Your name and age group
✅ Quests you've completed
✅ Stickers you've earned
✅ Your gems total
✅ Your garden growth
✅ Your reflection responses

### Where Is It Saved?
📱 **On YOUR device only** (not on any server)
🔒 **Using your browser's local storage**
🚫 **Never sent anywhere** - completely private
💾 **Automatically saved** after each action

### Can I Delete My Data?
Yes! Here's how:
1. Go to **Browser Settings** → **Privacy/History**
2. Select **Clear Browsing Data**
3. Check **Cookies and cached files**
4. Your MindSpark Quest data will be deleted

### Can I Back Up My Data?
Currently, data is stored in your browser's local storage. To back up:
- Your data persists in your browser
- Export feature (coming soon): Will let you save progress as a file
- Try the application on the same browser/device

## Troubleshooting

### App Won't Open
**Problem**: Page shows blank or error
- Try: Hard refresh your browser (Ctrl+Shift+R / Cmd+Shift+R)
- Try: Clear browser cache
- Try: Open in a different browser
- Try: Check JavaScript is enabled in browser settings

### Progress Not Saving
**Problem**: Completed quests disappeared after page refresh
- Try: Make sure cookies are enabled
- Try: Check browser isn't in "private/incognito" mode
- Try: Clear browser cache and reload

### Characters Not Displaying
**Problem**: No SVG characters visible
- Try: Ensure JavaScript is enabled
- Try: Update your browser to latest version
- Try: Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)

### Story Text Not Readable
**Problem**: Small text or hard to read
- Try: Increase browser zoom (Ctrl+Plus or Cmd+Plus)
- Try: Adjust screen brightness
- Try: Try a larger screen/device
