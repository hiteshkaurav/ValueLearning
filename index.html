Offline Family Activity Cards

🔍 Curiosity Sensory Walk

Take a 10-minute quiet walk in a local park or your backyard. Challenge your child to identify 3 things they can hear, 3 they can see, and 3 they can smell or feel.

🤝 Mirror Dance Challenge

Face your child. One person is the leader and makes slow dance moves; the other person mirrors them exactly. This builds intense interdependence, empathy, and listening without talking.

🎨 Scribble Art Game

Draw a simple random scribble line on a blank piece of paper. Challenge your child to use their thinking flexibility to turn that scribble into a fully realized drawing of an animal or object!
